Swimming

My swimming career

  • I started swimming competitively when I was 9, in junior black. Near the end of that year I made my first qualification meet, LC regionals.
  • In my second year I was put into Age Group Green, were I made my first provincial time. The next year I stayed in the same group but worked harder in practice suddenly COVID hit.
  • The next year and a half we were online. Recently as the COVID restriction got lowered we were allowed to swim again.
  • I was put into the provincial group, and now I have made my first 3 junior provincal times. Very recently I have been training with the NKB national team, and I was invited to train during March Break.
  • Swimming strokes in order of best to worst: Freestyle, Fly, Breastroke, Backstroke.
